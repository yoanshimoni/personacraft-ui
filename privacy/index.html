Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension, LinkedIn AI Assistant, is designed to help users write better outreach messages on LinkedIn using AI-generated suggestions.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from your LinkedIn account.

User profile data (such as public LinkedIn names, job titles, summaries, and posts) is processed locally or securely sent to our AI server only for the purpose of generating a message suggestion. No data is stored after processing.

Third-Party Services

This extension may use external APIs (e.g. OpenAI or similar) for generating content. No personal credentials are shared with any third party.

Storage

All temporary data (like cached suggestions or user settings) is stored locally using chrome.storage on your device only.
